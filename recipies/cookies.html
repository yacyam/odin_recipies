Ginger Cookies

Description

A short 35 minute recipie with 20 minutes of prep time, and 10 to 15 minutes for cooking. 2 Dozen delicious cookies will be created and ready to share.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar (dough), 2 tablespoons (rolling)
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Ginger
  • Cinnamon
  • Cloves
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Margarine (Or Butter)
  • Eggs
  • Molasses

Steps

  1. Sift dry ingredients together
  2. Cream margarine and sugar together. Beat in the egg, then add the water and molasses
  3. Stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until well-combined
  4. Shape the dough into balls, roll the balls in sugar, and flatten them lightly
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until almost set and coll on a wire rack
